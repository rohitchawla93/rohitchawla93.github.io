Teaching

Dynamics teaching with physical demonstrations.

I like teaching dynamics through a mix of theory, computation, experiments and mechanisms students can see, build, measure and question.

Applied Dynamics

Undergraduate teaching in particle kinematics, kinetics, rigid-body dynamics, planetary motion, mass flow and mechanical systems.

Experimental demonstrations

Chain fountain, gyroscopes, rotating platforms, elevator acceleration using smartphone sensors and mechanical analog circuits.

Student projects

Mechanisms, animating machines, mechanical clocks, circus dynamics and hands-on exploration of dynamics beyond formula memorisation.
